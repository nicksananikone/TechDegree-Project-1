My Interests

Let's DIVE into things that make me happy!

Rescuing Turtles

Turtles happen to be one of the most beautiful creatures to walk and swim this planet. All they want to do is grace our oceans with their presence, yet many people still think it's okay to pollute their habitats. I'm making it my mission to save ALL of the turtles!

Thrift Shopping

Finding vintage clothing at thrift stores has been one of my go-to interests over the past year and a half. I find so much joy "treasure hunting" for vintage clothes that could be valuable. I also believe having an eye for this sort of thing shaped my style today!
